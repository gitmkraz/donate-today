Food for a family

Pack your go bag with non-perishable food such as granola bars, biscuits, cereal, instant oatmeal, peanut butter, Vegemite, crackers, long-life milk, tinned food (tuna, fruit, soup, vegetables) and a can opener. Pack enough bottles of water for your family. If you have room, pack plastic plates and cutlery.
